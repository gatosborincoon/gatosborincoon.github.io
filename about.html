This organization to save cats was founded upon discovering this little creature, Leo in the State Forest in Puerto Rico. After discovering he and so many other many other cats in Puerto Rico are abandoned and lacking homes, I decided something must be done to save some of them. By partnering with Flight Angels, local vets, and volunteers we work to rehome the diverse population of abandoned cats in Puerto Rico to loving homes.
